Here are a few activities we have had in the past. We thought you would enjoy seeing what kinds of things we have planned and carried out.

December 2022 was so much fun. We had a lovely dinner, and sisters read a Christmas book to help us get in the Christmas spirit.

January 2023, we renewed with three speakers who talked to us about making goals and reaching them. We learned to stretch ourselves physically, spiritually and mentally to have a more balanced life.

February 2023 was our service auction. Definitely a favorite. Sisters donated goods and services and we bid on them with points we earned through doing good things like scripture study, prayer and serving others.

March 2023, we learned how to make all kinds of cards with paint and other supplies

April 2023, we participated in a service project that benefited one of our neighbors. We prepared their landscape for planting and planted beautiful flowers.

May 2023, we made a star craft. It was so fun to see all the creativity of the sisters.

June 2023 was our summer block party. We played games and visited and ate yummy food.
