The purpose of Relief Society is to save souls and relieve suffering. To help accomplish this purpose, the Savage Mill Ward is planning activities monthly. These activities will happen at 7:00 pm, the first Thursday of every month. They are an opportunity to strengthen each other, share testimony, bring sisters closer to Jesus Christ, and serve others in our community. Our hope is that the sisters in our Relief Society will feel the love of the Savior, and the love of each other.
