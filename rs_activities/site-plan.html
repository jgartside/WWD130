Content

Home page

The purpose of Relief Society is to save souls and relieve suffering. To help accomplish this purpose, the Savage Mill Ward is planning activities monthly. These activities will happen at 7:00 pm, the first Thursday of every month. They are an opportunity to strengthen each other, share testimony, bring sisters closer to Jesus Christ, and serve others in our community. Our hope is that the sisters in our Relief Society will feel the love of the Savior, and the love of each other.

Here are a few activities we have had in the past. We thought you would enjoy seeing what kinds of things we have planned and carried out. December 2022 was so much fun. We had a lovely dinner, and sisters read a Christmas book to help us get in the Christmas spirit. January 2023, we renewed with three speakers that talked to us about making goals and reaching them. We learned to stretch ourselves physically, spiritually and mentally to have a more balanced life. February 2023 was our service auction. Definitely a favorite. Sisters donated goods and services and we bid on them with points we earned through doing good things like scripture study, prayer and serving others. April 2023, we participated in a service project that benefited one of our neighbors. We prepared their landscape for planting and planted beautiful flowers. May 2023, we made a star craft. It was so fun to see all the creativity of the sisters. June 2023 was our summer block party. We played games and visited and ate yummy food.

It can be indexing, or adding a memory or searching for family members that need their work done in the temple. We invite you to share what you have done on our ward Facebook page. December will be a celebration of the birth of the Savior. Join us as we remember Jesus Christ and the blessings we receive because He was born.
